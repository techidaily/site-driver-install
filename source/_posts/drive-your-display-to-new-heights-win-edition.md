**Way 1: Update the Graphics Driver via Device Manager**

Follow these steps:

 1\. Press **Win+R** (Windows logo key and R key) at the same time. A Run dialog box will appear.

 2\. Type **devmgmt.msc** in the run box and click the**OK** button.
