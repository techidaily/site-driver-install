4. Click**SEARCH** .  
![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/3090-ti-manual-3.jpg)

![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/05/3090-ti-manual-4.jpg)
