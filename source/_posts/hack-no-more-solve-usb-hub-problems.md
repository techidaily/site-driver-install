1. On your keyboard, press the **Windows logo key ![](https://images.drivereasy.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/img_5ae0331bc08e4.png)**  and**R** at the same time to invoke the**Run** box.
2. Type**devmgmt.msc** and click**OK** .
